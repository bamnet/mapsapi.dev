Privacy

tl: dr; We collect the smallest amount of information possible. The sensitive stuff stays locally in your browser.

We collect the minimum information needed to provide this service, including your IP address and User Agent. This information is sent automatically by your browser used to fulfill your requests to the service and to build aggregated traffic reports.

We do not collect or store any configuration information about your Cloud Projects, API Keys, or other data made available after signing in to your cloud provider. This information only lives within your browser (client-side) and is not transmitted to our servers.

We do not use any third-party analytics or trackers and will not use your personally identifiable information for any purpose except as necessary to provide the service.

We have implemented technical security measures to protect the security of any information our service is granted access to. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, use of this website is at your own risk. This website should only be accessed from a secure browser environment.

If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@mapsapi.dev.
